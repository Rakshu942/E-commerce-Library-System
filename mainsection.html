About the Project


Grantha Lahari Portal is meticulously crafted to offer a comprehensive, user-friendly, and interactive platform for bibliophiles, enabling them to experience the essence of a physical library online. With robust features and secure access, it adeptly caters to the diverse needs of students, educators, researchers, and lifelong learners, ensuring a rich and engaging educational journey.This portal not only provides an extensive collection of books but also integrates innovative tools for a seamless reading experience. By bridging the gap between traditional and digital libraries, Grantha Lahari Portal stands as a beacon of modern education, making quality resources accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere.
